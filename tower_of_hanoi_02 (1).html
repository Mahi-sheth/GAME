Tower of Hanoi

A classic puzzle of recursive logic and data structures.

Rules of Play:

  1. Move all disks from the leftmost pole to the rightmost pole.
  2. Only one disk can be moved at a time.
  3. A disk must be taken from the top of a stack and placed on top of another.
  4. A larger disk cannot be placed on top of a smaller disk.
